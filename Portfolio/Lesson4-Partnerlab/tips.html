Essential Baking Tips

Always preheat your oven before baking to ensure even and thorough cooking.

  • Precision is key in baking! Always measure your ingredients carefully. Small measurement differences can dramatically change the final product, most professionals even recommend using a kitchen scale for accuracy.
  • Cold butter or eggs can affect texture and structure. Room temperature ingredients mix more evenly and create smoother batters. Make sure to check your recipies first to see if they call for room temperature/cold ingredients.
  • Avoid overmixing your batter. Overmixing develops gluten and can lead to dense cakes instead of light and fluffy textures.
