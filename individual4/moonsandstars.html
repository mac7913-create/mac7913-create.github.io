🌙 Moons & Stars of the Universe ⭐

The Bright Wonders of Space

Moons and stars are some of the most fascinating objects in the universe. Moons orbit planets while stars generate light and heat through nuclear fusion. Together, they help shape galaxies and solar systems.

🌙 Amazing Moons

Many planets have moons with unique environments and mysteries. Some may even contain underground oceans capable of supporting life.

  • Earth’s Moon – Controls tides and lights the night sky
  • Europa – Jupiter moon with a frozen ocean
  • Titan – Saturn moon with lakes of methane
  • Ganymede – Largest moon in the solar system
  • Io (see right) – Most volcanically active with hundreds of volcanoes

⭐ Powerful Stars

Stars are giant spheres of hot plasma that shine across the universe. The Sun is our closest star and provides the we energy need for life + heat and light throughout the system.

  1. Stars form inside nebulae (seen left)
  2. They produce energy through fusion
  3. Massive stars can become supernovae
  4. Some stars collapse into black holes
